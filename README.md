
AI Block for YouTube

a small firefox add-on for people who are tired of ai slop everywhere

It flags channels for easy view of their nature or lets you hide them outright, and gives you a quick way to report new channels for review so that we can work together to bin the slop.

AI content is contributing to the death of the human internet, and I'm sick of watching platforms fill up with synthetic slop. This add-on is one small pushback agaisnt that. 

More info at https://deadinternet.co.uk/ and you can download it directly from the addon store at https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/ai-block-for-youtube/
